Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need improv experience to audition?

No! In fact, most of us started with zero improv experience. We’ll teach you everything you need to know!

What is an improv audition like?

Improv auditions mimic the rehearsals we have year round. We play a few warm up games and a few games that include some scene work. All in all, it’s a fun, low pressure environment and a great place to laugh and meet new people!

Who is eligible to audition?

Any Yale undergraduate student!
